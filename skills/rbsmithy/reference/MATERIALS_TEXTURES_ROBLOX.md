# Materials and Textures for Roblox

Roblox often rewards simple, readable materials more than complex PBR stacks.

Use clear color groups, roughness/metalness only where useful, texture sizes appropriate to gameplay camera, material consistency across asset families, and stylized color palettes.

Avoid giant unique 4K textures for small props, too many material slots, tiny details that disappear, shader setups that do not translate to Roblox, and over-realistic materials in stylized games.

## Texture atlasing

Use atlases when many props share one style, especially crates, barrels, planks, signs, modular walls, roof pieces, and ruin blocks.
